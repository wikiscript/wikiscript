'''Sankt Pölten''' ({{IPA-de|zaŋkt ˈpœltn̩|-|De-at St Pölten.ogg}}), mostly abbreviated to the official name '''St. Pölten''',<ref>{{Cite web|url=https://www.ris.bka.gv.at/GeltendeFassung.wxe?Abfrage=LrNO&Gesetzesnummer=20000101|title=St. Pöltner Stadtrecht 1977|accessdate=February 28, 2015 }}</ref> is the capital and largest city of the [[States of Austria|State]] of [[Lower Austria]] in northeast Austria, with 52,716 inhabitants as of 1 January 2015. St Pölten is a city with its own statute (or ''[[Statutarstadt]]'') and therefore it is both a municipality and a district in the ''[[Mostviertel]]''.
